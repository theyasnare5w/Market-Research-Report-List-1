<p><p>The suborbital space tourism market features two main types: rocket-powered spaceplanes and traditional rockets. They provide a smoother flight experience and easier access for tourists. In contrast, traditional rockets are vertical-launch vehicles that ascend rapidly to the edge of space before returning, offering a thrilling experience but with a more intense launch and re-entry. Both types cater to the growing demand for adventure travel beyond Earth’s atmosphere.</p></p>
