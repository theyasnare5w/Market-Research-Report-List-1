<p><p>The cladding service market in metalworking encompasses various techniques used to bond different materials for enhanced performance. Roll bonding is a method where two metals are pressed together and passed through rollers to create a cohesive layer. Explosive welding uses controlled detonations to fuse metals, achieving strong bonds even with dissimilar materials. Laser cladding employs a laser beam to melt a material powder onto a substrate, providing a fine, precise coating that improves wear resistance and durability. Each method serves unique industrial needs.</p></p>
