<p><p>The fermented dairy product bacteria culture market is primarily categorized into mesophilic and thermophilic bacteria types. Conversely, thermophilic bacteria function at higher temperatures, ideal for making products like mozzarella and Swiss cheese, enhancing their distinct characteristics. Both types play crucial roles in the fermentation process, affecting the quality, taste, and nutritional value of dairy products, driving their market demand.</p></p>
