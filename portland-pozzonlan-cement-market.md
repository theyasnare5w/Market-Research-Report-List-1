<p><p>The Portland Pozzolanic Cement market is divided into two main types: natural and artificial pozzolana. Natural pozzolana is derived from volcanic ash or other natural sources, offering enhanced durability and performance in concrete applications. Conversely, artificial pozzolana is produced through industrial processes, such as the calcination of clay or fly ash, providing controlled properties and consistency. Both types improve the strength and longevity of cement while contributing to sustainable construction practices by reducing the environmental impact of traditional cement production.</p></p>
