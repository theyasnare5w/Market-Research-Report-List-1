<p><p>The thin light box market primarily comprises three lighting types: LED, EEFL (External Electrode Fluorescent Lamp), and T4 fluorescent bulbs. LED lights are energy-efficient, offering longer lifespans and vibrant colors, making them popular for modern applications. EEFLs provide higher efficacy and brightness with a compact design, often used in commercial displays. T4 fluorescent bulbs, known for their slender profile, deliver reliable illumination at lower costs, frequently utilized in various retail and signage setups. Each type enhances visibility and visual appeal in thin light boxes.</p></p>
