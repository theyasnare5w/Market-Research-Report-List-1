<p><p>Closed loop stepper motors are categorized into three main types: Permanent Magnet, Hybrid, and Variable Reluctance. Permanent Magnet stepper motors use permanent magnets to produce torque, offering reliable performance and ease of control. Hybrid stepper motors combine features of both permanent magnet and variable reluctance designs, providing improved torque and precision. Variable Reluctance stepper motors operate based on magnetic reluctance changes, delivering simple construction and high-speed performance. Together, these types cater to various applications requiring precise motion control and efficiency.</p></p>
