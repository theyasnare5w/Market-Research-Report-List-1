<p><ul><li>Spring</li><li>Rubber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Seat Shock Absorber market primarily consists of two types: spring and rubber shock absorbers. Spring shock absorbers utilize metal coils to absorb and convert kinetic energy into mechanical energy, providing a smooth ride by reducing vibrations. In contrast, rubber shock absorbers leverage the elastic properties of rubber to dampen shocks and vibrations, offering flexibility and durability. Both types are essential in enhancing passenger comfort and safety in vehicles by improving ride quality and minimizing the impact of road irregularities.</p></p>
