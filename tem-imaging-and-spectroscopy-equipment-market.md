<p><p>TEM Imaging and Spectroscopy Equipment encompasses various techniques to analyze materials at the nanoscale. Electron Energy Loss Spectroscopy (EELS) provides insights into electronic and chemical properties by measuring energy lost by electrons as they pass through a specimen. Energy-filtered Transmission Electron Microscopy (EFTEM) utilizes an energy filter to enhance image contrast based on specific elemental information. Other methods include Scanning Transmission Electron Microscopy (STEM) and diffraction techniques, each offering unique capabilities for material characterization in research and industry applications.</p></p>
