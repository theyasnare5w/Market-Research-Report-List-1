<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electronic Toll Collection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rfid-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Dsrc-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Video Analytic-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Gnss/Gps-Based ETC Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic toll collection (ETC) market includes several systems: </p><p>1. **RFID-Based ETC Systems** use radio-frequency identification tags for automatic toll collection.</p><p>2. **DSRC-Based ETC Systems** rely on dedicated short-range communication for real-time data exchange between vehicles and toll booths.</p><p>3. **Video Analytic-Based ETC Systems** capture vehicle images for license plate recognition, enabling toll processing without physical tags.</p><p>4. **GNSS/GPS-Based ETC Systems** utilize satellite positioning to charge tolls based on vehicle location, offering flexibility for all-road tolling solutions.</p></p>
