<p><p>The ventilated stretch film market consists of two main types: perforated manual film and perforated machine film. Perforated manual film is designed for hand application, featuring small holes that allow airflow, making it suitable for wrapping perishable goods and preventing moisture buildup. Perforated machine film, on the other hand, is intended for automated wrapping systems, offering efficiency and consistency while also facilitating air circulation. Both types enhance product preservation and reduce spoilage, catering to various industrial and retail needs.</p></p>
