<p><p>The pain relievers for the teeth market primarily includes two types: NSAIDs (Non-Steroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drugs) and central pain relievers. NSAIDs, such as ibuprofen and naproxen, effectively reduce inflammation and alleviate pain by blocking specific enzymes in the body. In contrast, central pain relievers, like acetaminophen, work on the central nervous system to alter the perception of pain without addressing inflammation. Both types play crucial roles in managing dental pain, providing relief and improving patients' quality of life.</p></p>
