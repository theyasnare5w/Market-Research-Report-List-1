<p><p>Laboratory Management Software (LMS) is categorized into three main market types: on-premise, cloud-based, and remotely hosted solutions. On-premise software is installed locally on laboratory servers, offering high control but requiring significant maintenance. Cloud-based LMS allows users to access the software via the internet, providing flexibility, scalability, and reduced IT overhead. Remotely hosted solutions balance between the two, where the software runs on external servers managed by a third party, ensuring accessibility while minimizing local resource demands.</p></p>
