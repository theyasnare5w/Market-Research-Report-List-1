<p><p>The Bariatric Trolley Market comprises four main types: Manual, Hydraulic, Electric, and Hydro-pneumatic. Manual trolleys rely on physical effort for adjustments, offering basic mobility. Hydraulic trolleys utilize fluid pressure for smooth height adjustments, enhancing patient accessibility. Electric trolleys are powered by motors, providing effortless operation and precise positioning, ideal for high-demand settings. Hydro-pneumatic trolleys combine both hydraulic and pneumatic systems, offering superior stability and versatility, making them suitable for a range of healthcare environments where bariatric care is essential.</p></p>
