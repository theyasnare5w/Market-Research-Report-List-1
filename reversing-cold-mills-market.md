<p><p>Reversing cold mills are categorized into two main types: single-stand and two-stand mills. Single-stand reversing cold mills feature one rolling stand, allowing coils to pass through the mill in both directions, making them efficient for small to medium production volumes. In contrast, two-stand reversing cold mills consist of two stands arranged in tandem, enhancing production capacity and enabling thicker materials to be processed. Both types are crucial for producing high-quality flat products and improving metal properties through cold rolling.</p></p>
