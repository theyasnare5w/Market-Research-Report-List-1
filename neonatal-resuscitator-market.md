<p><p>The neonatal resuscitator market is primarily divided into two segments: manual resuscitators and automatic resuscitators. Manual resuscitators are hand-operated devices that provide positive pressure ventilation and require skilled personnel for effective use, making them essential in emergency situations. In contrast, automatic resuscitators are automated systems designed to deliver consistent ventilation and reduce the likelihood of human error, enhancing efficiency and safety. Both types are crucial in neonatal care, ensuring timely and effective respiratory support for newborns in distress.</p></p>
