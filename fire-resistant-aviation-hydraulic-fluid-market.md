<p><p>The Fire Resistant Aviation Hydraulic Fluid market is categorized into four main types: HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are water-containing solutions with excellent fire resistance. HFB fluids are also water-containing but have higher viscosity and thermal stability. HFC fluids are non-water-containing, synthetic fluids that maintain performance at elevated temperatures, while HFD fluids are a category of high-density, non-water-containing, synthetic fluids designed for extreme fire resistance in aviation applications. Each type serves specific operational needs based on fire safety and performance.</p></p>
