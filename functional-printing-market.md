<p><p>The functional printing market encompasses various methods, each suited to specific applications. Screen printing, known for its durability, is ideal for textiles and signage. Gravure printing excels in high-volume production of intricate designs, often used in packaging. Flexography offers versatility for printing on various substrates, commonly utilized in labels and wrappers. Inkjet printing provides precision and adaptability for short runs and personalized items. Additionally, other techniques such as offset and digital printing cater to niche applications, expanding the market's versatility.</p></p>
