<p><p>The single wafer cleaning tools market is segmented into dry and wet process cleaning tools. Dry process cleaning tools utilize gas-phase reagents to remove contaminants without using liquids, ensuring minimal waste and faster processing times, suitable for sensitive materials. Wet process cleaning tools, on the other hand, employ liquid solutions to effectively dissolve and wash away particles and contaminants from wafer surfaces. Both methods are essential for maintaining the integrity of semiconductor manufacturing and enhancing device performance.</p></p>
