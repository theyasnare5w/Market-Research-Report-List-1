<p><p>DNA analysis in the government sector focuses on various market types for forensic, identification, and regulatory purposes. Restriction Fragment Length Polymorphism (RFLP) detects variations in DNA sequences, aiding forensic investigations. Short Tandem Repeat (STR) analysis is commonly used in criminal cases for individual identification. Single Nucleotide Polymorphism (SNP) analysis provides insights into genetic variations affecting populations or individuals. Other methods may include mitochondrial DNA analysis or whole genome sequencing, enhancing capabilities in law enforcement, public health, and biodiversity monitoring.</p></p>
