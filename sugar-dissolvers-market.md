<p><strong>The Sugar Dissolvers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous</li><li>Batch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sugar dissolvers market is segmented into two main types: continuous and batch systems. Continuous sugar dissolvers operate by steadily feeding sugar into a constant flow of water, ensuring a consistent and rapid dissolution process, ideal for large-scale production. In contrast, batch sugar dissolvers mix a pre-defined amount of sugar and water in discrete batches, allowing for flexibility and variations in formulation. Each type serves specific industrial needs, with continuous systems favored for efficiency and batch systems for customization.</p></p>
