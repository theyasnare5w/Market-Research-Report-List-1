<p><p>The solvent recycling system market is segmented into batch processing and continuous processing. Batch processing involves treating fixed quantities of solvents at specific intervals, making it ideal for smaller operations or varied solvent types. In contrast, continuous processing enables the ongoing treatment of solvents, allowing for higher efficiency and reduced downtime, suitable for larger-scale operations that handle consistent solvent types. Both methods aim to enhance sustainability by reusing solvents, reducing waste and operational costs in various industries.</p></p>
