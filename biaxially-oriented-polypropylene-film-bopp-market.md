The growing demand for sustainable and recyclable materials has further propelled the popularity of BOPP films in diverse industries.</p><p>The BOPP market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increased consumption in the packaging sector, particularly in food and beverages, where its moisture and grease resistance is highly valued.
