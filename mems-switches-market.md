<p><strong>The MEMS Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Switch</li><li>Series Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) switches are classified into two main market types: shunt switches and series switches. Shunt switches connect a signal to the ground, effectively diverting it and enabling signal routing in communication systems. They are often used in RF applications for their low insertion loss. Series switches, in contrast, allow signals to pass straight through while maintaining their path, making them crucial in applications requiring high bandwidth and minimal signal distortion, such as in telecommunications and data centers.</p></p>
