<p><ul><li>Single-Level</li><li>Multi-Level</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Baggage carousel markets can be categorized into single-level and multi-level systems. Single-level markets feature a single, horizontal conveyor belt where luggage is delivered for retrieval, commonly seen in smaller airports. In contrast, multi-level markets utilize vertically stacked carousels, enabling efficient use of space and quicker access to luggage in larger terminals. Both systems aim to streamline the baggage handling process, enhance passenger convenience, and optimize airport operations, ultimately improving the overall travel experience.</p></p>
