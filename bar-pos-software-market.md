<p><ul><li>Web-Based</li><li>Installed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bar POS software is categorized into two main market types: web-based and installed. Web-based POS systems operate through the internet, allowing access from any device with a browser. They offer flexibility and remote management but require a stable internet connection. Installed POS systems are software downloaded onto specific devices, providing local control and often enhanced performance without internet dependency. Both types cater to different operational needs, with web-based solutions ideal for multi-location management and installed systems preferred for single-site establishments.</p></p>
