<p><p>The Thoracic Aortic Stent Graft System (TAA Stent Graft System) is designed to treat diseases of the thoracic aorta. The market is segmented into two main types: unibody and bifurcated stent grafts. Unibody stent grafts consist of a single continuous structure, providing streamlined deployment and reduced risk of complications. In contrast, bifurcated stent grafts feature a branched design to accommodate the aorta's natural bifurcation, facilitating more effective treatment for complex aortic conditions involving branch vessels.</p></p>
