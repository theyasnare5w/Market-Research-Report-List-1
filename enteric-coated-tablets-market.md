<p><p>Enteric-coated tablets are pharmaceutical formulations designed with a special coating that prevents the tablet from dissolving in the acidic environment of the stomach. Instead, they dissolve in the more neutral pH of the intestines, allowing for better absorption of certain medications while protecting the stomach lining from potential irritation. These tablets are particularly useful for delivering drugs that are unstable in gastric acid or for medications that need to release active ingredients in the intestines.</p><p>The Enteric-coated Tablets Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.7% during the forecast period, driven by several factors. Increasing prevalence of gastrointestinal disorders and the demand for targeted drug delivery systems contribute significantly to market expansion. Additionally, the growing geriatric population, which often requires specialized medications, fuels the need for enteric coatings. Innovations in tablet formulation and manufacturing techniques, along with the rise in consumer awareness about the benefits of enteric-coated products, further enhance market growth.
