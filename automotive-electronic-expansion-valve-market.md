<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Electronic Expansion Valve</li><li>Electric Electronic Expansion Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Electronic Expansion Valve market is segmented into two main types: Electromagnetic Electronic Expansion Valves and Electric Electronic Expansion Valves. Electromagnetic valves utilize magnetic forces to control the flow of refrigerants based on varying temperature and pressure conditions, offering precise regulation. In contrast, Electric Electronic Expansion Valves employ electric signals to adjust the valve position, enabling highly responsive and energy-efficient operation. Both types contribute to enhanced thermal management and overall system efficiency in modern automotive climate control systems.</p></p>
