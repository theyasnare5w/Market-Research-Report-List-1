<p><strong>The Single Axis Joystick Handle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Effect Joystick Handle</li><li>Potentiometer Joystick Handle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Single Axis Joystick Handle market comprises two main types: Hall Effect Joystick Handles and Potentiometer Joystick Handles. Hall Effect Joystick Handles utilize magnetic sensors to detect position, offering high precision, durability, and resistance to wear. In contrast, Potentiometer Joystick Handles employ variable resistors to measure position, which can lead to wear over time but are typically more cost-effective. Both types are widely used in various applications, including gaming, industrial controls, and robotics, each catering to specific performance and operational needs.</p></p>
