<p><p>Gas density sensors are crucial in monitoring gas concentrations in various environments. The market is segmented into point sensors and open path sensors. Point sensors are localized devices that assess gas concentration at a specific spot, making them ideal for targeted monitoring in confined spaces. In contrast, open path sensors measure gas density over a lengthy path, allowing for broader area surveillance and detection of leaks. Both types serve distinct applications in industries such as environmental monitoring, safety, and process control.</p></p>
