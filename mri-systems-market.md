<p><p>The MRI systems market is primarily categorized into open MRI systems and closed MRI systems. Open MRI systems feature a wider opening, offering greater patient comfort and accessibility, which is ideal for those with claustrophobia or larger body sizes. Closed MRI systems, on the other hand, provide higher-resolution imaging due to stronger magnets and enclosed environments, making them the preferred choice for detailed diagnostic purposes. Both types serve unique patient needs and vary in technological capabilities and applications.</p></p>
