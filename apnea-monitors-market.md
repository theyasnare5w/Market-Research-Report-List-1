<p><strong>The Apnea Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel Screening Devices</li><li>Actigraphy Systems</li><li>PSG Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The apnea monitors market comprises various types, including single-channel screening devices, actigraphy systems, and PSG (polysomnography) devices. Single-channel devices are compact tools that monitor one aspect of breathing during sleep, making them cost-effective for initial screenings. Actigraphy systems track sleep patterns and movements to infer apnea events over time, providing a broader view of sleep quality. PSG devices offer comprehensive assessments by recording multiple physiological parameters, making them the gold standard for diagnosing sleep apnea and related disorders.</p></p>
