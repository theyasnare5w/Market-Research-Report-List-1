<p><p>The Steel Cord Rubber Conveyor Belts market primarily consists of two types: Strand Steel Cord Rubber Conveyor Belts and Layer Steel Cord Rubber Conveyor Belts. Strand Steel Cord belts utilize individual steel wires that are twisted together to enhance strength and durability, ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, Layer Steel Cord belts feature multiple layers of steel cords, providing increased load capacity and flexibility. Both types are designed for conveying bulk materials across various industrial settings, ensuring efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
