<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart sleep monitoring equipment market consists of two main types: contact and contactless devices. Contact devices typically involve wearable technology, such as smartwatches or fitness bands, that track sleep patterns by measuring biometric data through sensors placed on the skin. In contrast, contactless devices use non-invasive methods like infrared sensors or cameras to monitor sleep from a distance, analyzing movement and environmental factors without requiring direct contact with the user. Both types aim to enhance sleep quality and provide insights for better health.</p></p>
