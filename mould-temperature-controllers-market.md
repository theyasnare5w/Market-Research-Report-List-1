<p><p>Mould temperature controllers are essential in maintaining optimal temperature during the molding process, ensuring quality and consistency in production. The market is primarily categorized into oil type and water type controllers. Oil type controllers use thermal oil for heat transfer, offering higher stability and higher temperature capabilities, making them suitable for demanding applications. Conversely, water type controllers utilize water as a coolant, providing efficient heat transfer at lower temperatures, ideal for standard applications. Both types cater to different manufacturing needs and environments.</p></p>
